# Design Doc of "Tank me LoL"

*Core Loop*: Surviving a 3D labyrinth by killing enemies and collecting upgrades
*Goal*: To survive as long as possible
*Challenge*: Enemies actively oppose the player, trying to kill them when possible
*Feedback*: Player always knows what level they are, how long they survived and now many points did they get
*Progress*: More geometric levels, upgrades, new weapons and mechanics, new enemies

*Theme*: Mini worlds? Vaprires in tanks survivors
*Setting*: Floating world, dimension of tank
*What do you collect*: Upgrades
*New Mechanic*: The map is located on the outer surface of the cube and the player moves along different sides of it
*How does it work*: The player's camera is aligned with the current plane and changes depending on their plane
